My FAV Girl

Elsa was the princess of the kingdom of Arendelle, born with a strange and rare magical power—she could create ice and snow. As a child, she was very close to her little sister Anna, but after accidentally hurting Anna while playing with her magic, her parents decided to isolate her from Anna to protect her. Elsa spent many years locked in her room, afraid of her magic and keeping it hidden inside, until both parents died during a sea voyage..
